Economical AI Execution

Modern enterprises face serious challenges with rising token consumption, surging system demand, and expensive public frontier model inference costs. Cognitech resolves these friction points by architecting efficient, layered pipelines and purpose-built local models. We focus on right-sizing systems to ensure your operational frameworks remain safe, highly secure, and structurally economical.

We achieve this by customizing high-performing open-source foundations using Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) and Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA). By optimizing smaller, highly targeted open-weights models for narrow operational tasks, we eliminate dependency on expensive public cloud APIs, minimize latency, and drastically reduce long-term infrastructure spend.

Proof of Concept Spotlight: The BA-Agent Framework

Optimizing Delivery Frameworks for Logistics Infrastructure

Cognitech has engineered a specialized, context-aware hybrid architecture for a Business Analyst AI Agent, currently operating as a high-value Proof of Concept (PoC) scoped for a major enterprise leader in the logistics industry. The system connects with layered source frameworks and operational documentation, simplifying the logic of large code repositories and identifying complex technical dependencies.

Crucially, the architecture places the human specialist securely in control. The BA agent explicitly validates the generated User Stories and operational BA documentation directly with the human Business Analyst first. Only after manual confirmation by the analyst does the agent automatically push and publish the structured records into respective external tracking software like Jira and Confluence.

This model is engineered to complement internal structures—systematically scaling team capability and transforming regular technical analysis into rapid execution.
